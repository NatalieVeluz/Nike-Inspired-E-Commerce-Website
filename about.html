ABOUT US

NIKE Incorporated

NIKE, Inc. is a collective consisting of the Nike, Jordan, and Converse brands united by a common goal to make a lasting and meaningful influence. With a global footprint, culture of innovation and team-first mentality, we take action to create a future of continual progress for athletes, sport and our world.


Our company is more than just a brand; it's a global community of athletes, enthusiasts, and advocates for positive change. Our partnerships with athletes and teams around the world reflect our commitment to the transformative power of sport in uniting people and breaking down barriers.
